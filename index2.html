Biography

DigitalClassicist

I received a BA in Latin and Greek from Wright State, a PhD in Classical Humanities from the University of Florida, and have spent most of the last decade teaching. The lack of opportunities in working with dead languages, however, has inspired a move into digital languages. Computers and technology have long been a passion of mine, right alongside the ancient world, and I'm looking forward to spending the next several decades focusing on the former.
